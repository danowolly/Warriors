Golden State Warriors Stephen Curry

The son of Sonya and Dell Curry, Wardell Stephen Curry was Born in Akron, OH. His family then moved to Charlotte, NC where his father played for the Charlotte Hornets. Curry grew up playing basketball and continued playing at Davidson College until 2009.

He was drafted 7th overall by the Golden State Warriors and has been with the team ever since. He has many accolades inculding 2 MVP awards (one of them being unanimous), 3 championships, 8 all-star appearences, and holds the record of made threes with 3,117 and counting.
